Infertility treatments for women

  • PCOS / polycystic ovary syndrome
  • Endometriosis
  • Blocked fallopian tubes
  • Hydrosalpinx
  • Uterine fibroids
  • Adenomyosis
  • Genital / pelvic tuberculosis (TB)
  • Low AMH and reduced ovarian reserve
  • Advanced (elderly) maternal age
  • Recurrent abortions / recurrent pregnancy loss
  • Congenital uterine anomalies (septate uterus, bicornuate uterus)
  • Anovulation (eggs not forming / irregular or absent ovulation)

Male factor infertility

  • Azoospermia (no sperm in semen)
  • Oligozoospermia (low sperm count)
  • Teratozoospermia (abnormal sperm shape / morphology)
  • Combined and unexplained male factor infertility

Hystero‑laparoscopy (hysteroscopy + laparoscopy) facility is available for diagnosis and treatment of complex infertility causes.
